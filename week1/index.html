  • When in Javascript, use .value whenever you are getting input from the user. Use .innerHTML when you are going to display something.
  • Use a section tag to display any output to a user.

Mobile First Design Strategy

  • Keep in mind how people hold their phones.
  • 49% of people use only one hand. Try to place things within easy reach, so they can get to it without having to adjust their grip.
  • Speed matters. Reduce image usage, use SVG or webfonts, optimze and minimize CSS an JS files, GZIP your files.
  • Mobile friendly forms:
    • only ask for absolutely necessary information
    • ditch labels and put the label inside the text box
    • taller boxes are easier for fingers to tap
    • paginate or make the form grow depending on what and how much information you need
    • enable autofill
    • bring up the most efficient keyboard
  • Link phone numbers using "phone number"
